what bliss does

everything you need,
nothing you don't.

🛡️
Ping Protection
5-stage escalation ladder — warning, timeout, longer timeout, kick, and finally ban — for users who won't stop pinging protected members.
✨
Fake Nitro
Use nitro-only emojis and stickers without paying a cent. Bliss sends them as a webhook using your exact avatar and display name.
🎨
Color Roles
Give yourself a personalized hex color cosmetic role. No permissions, just vibes. Updates live if you change your mind.
🔥
Channel Purge
Mods can wipe an entire channel instantly via clone-and-delete — no 14-day message limit, no bulk-delete cap.
👋
Auto-Role
Automatically assign a role to every new member who joins. Set it once and forget it.
😴
Ping Easter Egg
Bliss responds to pings with snarky sleepy messages. Dare to ping twice after the warning and your nickname gets changed to something humbling.
